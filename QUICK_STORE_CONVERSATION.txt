# Quick Store Implementation - Conversation Transcript
## DTF Layout Project

---

## CONTEXT

Alok is building DTF Layout, a SaaS platform for DTF printing businesses. This conversation covers implementing the "Quick Store" feature - mini-websites for printers without their own websites.

---

## WHAT WAS DECIDED

### Feature Overview
- **Quick Store**: Hosted mini-storefronts at `{store-slug}.dtflayout.com`
- **Purpose**: Printers without websites can receive orders from customers
- **Flow**: Customer designs sheet → Submits order → Printer downloads (credits deducted) → Fulfills offline

### Key Technical Decisions
1. **URL**: Subdomain-based (`mumbai-prints.dtflayout.com`)
2. **Pricing**: Flexible system
   - Per length OR per area
   - Flat OR tiered rates
   - 7 currencies (INR, USD, EUR, GBP, AED, AUD, CAD)
   - 4 measurement units (inch, cm, meter, feet)
   - Slab OR incremental tier calculation
3. **Credits**: Single balance across all DTF Layout products
4. **Customer**: Anonymous (only name + phone at checkout)
5. **Order Flow**: Submit → Pending → Downloaded (credits deducted) → Completed
6. **Rate Limiting**: 50 orders/store/day, 10 orders/phone/day
7. **Storage**: Tiered limits with auto-cleanup

### Database Tables Created
- `quick_stores` - Store identity, contact, appearance, settings
- `quick_store_testimonials` - Customer reviews
- `quick_store_products` - Product catalog with pricing config
- `quick_store_orders` - Customer orders
- `quick_store_analytics` - Event tracking
- `reserved_slugs` - System-reserved names
